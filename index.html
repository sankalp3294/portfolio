About Me

Hello! I'm Sankalp Sahare, a Computer Science Engineering student with a strong interest in Full-Stack Web Development, UI/UX Design, and Artificial Intelligence. I enjoy transforming ideas into practical digital solutions that combine functionality with intuitive user experiences.

My journey began with a Diploma in Computer Engineering, where I developed a solid foundation in programming, database management, and software development. Currently I am pursuing my degree in Computer Science and Engineering, and I have worked on projects ranging from web-based applications to AI-driven solutions, gaining hands-on experience with Python, Flask, MySQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Figma.

I have contributed to technical communities as a former member of Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC), former Designing Lead at COSMA Forum, and former Publicity Club member. These experiences helped me strengthen my leadership, communication, and teamwork skills alongside my technical abilities.

I have also completed a Web Development Internship at WideSoftech Pvt. Ltd., where I gained practical industry exposure and experience working on real-world development tasks. Currently, I am focused on enhancing my expertise in Full-Stack Development, UI/UX Design, AI/ML, and modern web technologies while building impactful projects and seeking opportunities to grow as a software developer.

Beyond technology, I enjoy sketching, creative design, and exploring innovative ways to solve real-world problems through technology.
